
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: We may consider using git because it allows us to track changes over time, collaborate without overwriting code, and also go back to older versions if something breaks while working on something new.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines two branches and usually creates a merge commit, while rebasing replays commits on top of another branch to keep history more linear
